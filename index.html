Our AIM

Vision

Creating a platform where students would get a chance to interact with researchers and industrial people in the respective fields of their interest.

Mission

Advancing Technology for Humanity and ensuring a true bond of commitment towards a better future.

Activities

Conducting various events like seminars, workshops, paper presentations, and tech quizzes, where students work, cooperate, coordinate and compete to, develop their intellectual as well as professional skills.

IEEE Student Branch

IEEE's mission has always been advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. Our student chapter aims to spread technical awareness among students and all other indivials interested in learning about what the future holds for us.

We collaborate with experienced and inspiring speakers from different part of our country as well as across the globe, who help us accomplish IEEEs vision of serving as educators in our community of aspiring engineers and scientists.
