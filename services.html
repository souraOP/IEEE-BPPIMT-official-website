One day Symposium on ChatGPT: Opportunities and Challenges For Education

IEEE-BPPIMT along with IEEE COMSOC, Kolkata Section is thrilled to announce an upcoming event on One Day Symposium on ChatGPT: Opportunities and Challenges for Education! This event aims to explore the revolutionary impact of ChatGPT, an advanced language model developed by OpenAI, on the field of education. The symposium will provide a platform for educators, researchers, and technology enthusiasts to delve into the potential applications, benefits, and challenges of integrating ChatGPT into educational settings.

We are honored to have two distinguished speakers leading the discussions on this groundbreaking topic. The first speaker, Professor Chiranjib Bhattacharyya, hails from the prestigious Indian Institute of Science (IISc) in Bangalore. With his extensive expertise in artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and machine learning, Prof. Bhattacharyya has made significant contributions to the field.

Joining Professor Swagatam Das from the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) in Kolkata. Prof. Das is renowned for his research in evolutionary algorithms, optimization, and pattern recognition. With his comprehensive knowledge and experience in these domains, he will shed light on the challenges and ethical considerations associated with integrating ChatGPT into educational contexts.

Throughout the symposium, participants will have the opportunity to engage in thought-provoking discussions, explore case studies, and exchange ideas with fellow professionals. By the end of the event, attendees will gain a deeper understanding of the potential benefits and pitfalls of employing ChatGPT in education, enabling them to make informed decisions and shape the future of technology-enhanced learning.

We are thrilled to welcome you all to this insightful symposium, where we will collectively explore the transformative possibilities and challenges that ChatGPT presents for education.

Delve into GSoC

Join us for an insightful session, "Delve into Google Summer of Code," where we are honored to have Sunit Roy, a distinguished speaker who has made significant contributions to the world of open-source development through his participation in Google Summer of Code (GSoC) at TensorFlow.

The Google Summer of Code program has long been a vital platform for nurturing and empowering aspiring developers worldwide. It provides a unique opportunity for talented individuals to collaborate with leading open-source organizations, gain hands-on experience, and contribute to cutting-edge projects.

In this event, we are excited to welcome Sunit Roy, an exceptional GSoC alumnus who was selected to work with TensorFlow, the renowned open-source library for machine learning. Sunit's remarkable journey and outstanding accomplishments during his GSoC experience make him an ideal speaker to shed light on the program and inspire others to follow in his footsteps.

During the event, Sunit will delve into various aspects of the Google Summer of Code program, including the application process, project selection, mentorship, and community engagement.

Don't miss this unique opportunity to hear from Sunit Roy, a remarkable GSoC alumnus who made his mark with TensorFlow. Join us for "Delve into Google Summer of Code" and get ready to be inspired on 19th of May. Reserve your spot now and take the first step towards an exciting future in open-source development!

Basics of C++ Workshop

IEEE-BPPIMT is thrilled to announce an upcoming event focused on the programming language C++. The event will cover all the basics of programming, as well as the basics and moderate levels of C++.

C++ is a powerful programming language that is widely used in software development, gaming, and system programming. It is essential for aspiring programmers to learn C++ as it is the foundation of many popular programming languages like Java, Python, and Ruby.

During the event, attendees will be able to learn the fundamentals of programming, such as data types, variables, and control structures. Additionally, the event will provide a comprehensive introduction to the C++ programming language, including syntax, data structures, abstract classes and also bits object-oriented programming.

The event is open to all, regardless of experience level, making it a great opportunity for those new to programming as well as those who are looking to enhance their skills.

Our Core Members will be the speakers for this event and will guide you through!

Inauguration Program

The inauguration program of IEEE-BPPIMT will be a significant event that will mark the establishment of the student chapter of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) at B.P. Poddar Institute of Management and Technology (BPPIMT).

This program is going to witness the presence of several eminent personalities from the industry and academia who will grace the occasion and shared their valuable insights on the importance of IEEE and its role in promoting technical knowledge among students. This event includes a series of interactive sessions and workshops that aim to provide the students with a platform to learn and explore the latest trends and developments in the field of engineering.

We have Sayak Paul as our guest Speaker who is going to talk about Text to Image Generation using Diffusion Models.
We have Nita Patel: President of IEEE Computer Society and Eric Berkowitz: Director of Membership, IEEE Computer Society will be joining us online on our keynote session.
